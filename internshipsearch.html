Finding the Perfect Internship: Your Guide to Success

Searching for an internship can be both exciting and overwhelming. Whether you're looking to gain hands-on experience, explore career paths, or build your professional network, the right internship can be a game-changer. This guide is designed to help you navigate the internship search process with confidence and clarity.

From identifying your goals to acing the application process, we've got you covered. Let's dive in and make your internship search a successful journey!

Internships at UMSI

Transform classroom learning into real‑world impact.

Overview

Internships are a core part of the UMSI experience, giving students the opportunity to apply information skills in professional environments across UX, data science, libraries, health, policy, and more.

Why internships matter

  • Skill application: Put your coursework into practice through hands‑on projects.
  • Portfolio building: Create artifacts that demonstrate your process and growth.
  • Career exploration: Test industries and roles to refine your direction.
  • Professional connections: Build relationships with mentors and colleagues.

Tips for internship success

  • Set clear goals: Know what you want to learn.
  • Communicate proactively: Check in with supervisors.
  • Document your work: Save artifacts for your portfolio.
  • Stay connected: Maintain relationships after the internship.
